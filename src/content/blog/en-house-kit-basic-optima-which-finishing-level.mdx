At Tiny House Heavens, you can order each of our [20 models](/en/models/) in three finishing levels: **House Kit**, **Basic** and **Optima**. The difference? How ready your tiny house is to move into upon delivery. From a building kit for the DIY enthusiast to a fully turnkey home — each level has its advantages.

## Key takeaways

- **House Kit** is the most affordable and ideal for those who want to finish it themselves
- **Basic** is the most popular choice: fully finished, immediately liveable
- **Optima** is the worry-free option: everything included, right down to the lighting
- The price difference between House Kit and Optima averages **40% to 60%**
- Each level is available for [**all 20 models**](/en/models/)

---

## What is included in each finishing level?

### House Kit — The building kit

The House Kit is the foundation of every Tiny House Heavens home. You receive:

- **Steel undercarriage** — the load-bearing base of your tiny house
- **Timber frame construction** — the wall and roof structure
- **Insulated floor, walls and ceiling** — 150 to 200 mm insulation, depending on the model
- **Structural layout** — internal walls according to the chosen floor plan

**What is not included?**
- No exterior cladding or roofing
- No windows or doors
- No electricity, plumbing or heating
- No interior finish or flooring

**Who is it for?**
The House Kit is ideal if you are handy, if you hire a contractor, or if you want to tackle the finishing in phases. You save considerably on the purchase price and have complete freedom in choosing materials and finishes.

### Basic — The standard finish

The Basic finish delivers a **fully liveable home**. On top of the House Kit, you get:

- **Exterior cladding** — durable façade material
- **Metal roofing** — long-lasting and low maintenance
- **Windows and exterior doors** — double glazing
- **Basic electrics** — wiring, sockets and switches
- **Plumbing** — connection points for kitchen and bathroom
- **Interior finish** — walls and ceilings completed
- **Flooring** — standard floor installed

**What is not included?**
- No underfloor heating (preparation possible)
- No hot water boiler or ventilation system
- No lighting

**Who is it for?**
Basic is the most popular option. You can move in immediately and have a comfortable, fully finished home. Additional installations (heating, hot water) can be added later or installed by your own contractor.

### Optima — Turnkey

The Optima finish is the **complete package**. You get everything in Basic, plus:

- **Electric underfloor heating** — comfortable warmth without radiators
- **Hot water boiler** — instant hot water in kitchen and bathroom
- **Ventilation system** — healthy indoor climate with controlled ventilation
- **Full interior lighting** — all lights installed
- **Exterior lighting** — ambient lighting around your tiny house

**Who is it for?**
Optima is for those who want a **worry-free experience**. You receive the keys and can move in immediately without having to arrange anything else. This is the highest investment, but also the highest comfort.

## Which level best suits your situation?

### Choose House Kit if...
- You are handy or have a contractor
- You want to spread the budget over time
- You want complete freedom in material and design choices
- You have a limited starting budget

### Choose Basic if...
- You want to move into your tiny house as soon as possible
- You are looking for a good balance between price and comfort
- You may want to add extras later
- You do not want to do the finishing yourself

### Choose Optima if...
- You want a completely worry-free experience
- Comfort and convenience are your top priority
- You do not want any extra costs or works after delivery
- You want to enjoy underfloor heating, hot water and ventilation straight away

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Optima later?

Yes, in most cases it is possible to add underfloor heating, a hot water boiler or a ventilation system later. However, this is more expensive than choosing it at the time of ordering, as additional labour is required.

### Can I combine the finishing level with extra options?

Absolutely. Regardless of the chosen finishing level, you can add extra options such as solar panels, a heat pump, a sauna or a terrace. Discuss your wishes with our team.

### What is the price difference between the levels?

The exact price difference depends on the model and size. On average, Optima is 40% to 60% higher than House Kit, with Basic in between. [Request a quote](/en/contact/) for tailored pricing.
