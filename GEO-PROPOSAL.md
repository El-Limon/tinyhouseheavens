### Critical Issues Found

#### Issue 1: Brand Is Invisible to AI Systems
**What we found:** Tiny House Heavens has zero presence on YouTube, Reddit, Wikipedia, LinkedIn, or any industry directory. The brand exists only on its own website plus Facebook and Instagram — platforms that have weak correlation with AI citations.
**Business impact:** When a potential customer asks ChatGPT "Who builds tiny houses in Belgium?" or "Best tiny house manufacturer Belgium?", your company will not appear in the answer. Competitors with even minimal external presence will be recommended instead.
**Our fix:** Systematic brand presence building across YouTube (model walkthrough videos), LinkedIn (company page + thought leadership), Google Business Profile (local AI queries), Reddit (community participation), and industry directories.
**Timeline:** Initial presence established within 30 days. Meaningful AI visibility within 90 days.

#### Issue 2: No Human Identity or Customer Proof
**What we found:** No individual team members, founders, or experts are named anywhere on the site. All blog posts credit the Organization, not a person. Zero customer reviews, testimonials, or case studies exist.
**Business impact:** AI systems use author credentials and social proof to decide which sources to trust and cite. Without named experts or verified customer experiences, your content loses credibility scoring against competitors — even those with technically inferior content.
**Our fix:** Establish founder/expert identity with Person schema, author pages, and professional credentials. Launch a systematic review collection program. Create 2-3 customer case studies with project photos and outcomes.
**Timeline:** Author identity established within 2 weeks. First testimonials within 30 days. Case studies within 60 days.

#### Issue 3: Content Lacks Cited Authority
**What we found:** Four blog posts covering cost, permits, buying guides, and finishing levels — all well-structured and informative — but containing zero external source citations. No government URLs, no industry data, no expert quotes.
**Business impact:** AI systems (especially Perplexity and ChatGPT with search) preferentially cite content that references verifiable external data. Your competitors who cite official Belgian permit portals or construction cost indices will be preferred as citation sources.
**Our fix:** Add authoritative source citations to all existing content. Establish a content production workflow that includes mandatory source citation. Publish original data from your own project delivery experience.
**Timeline:** Existing content updated within 2 weeks. New content production starts Month 1.
